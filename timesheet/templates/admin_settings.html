Time-off notifications

When an employee submits or cancels a time-off request, an email is sent to the address(es) below (if SMTP is configured). You can also post a message to Microsoft Teams.

Primary recipient(s). Use commas to add multiple emails. Leave blank to use the default from server config.
Optional. If enabled above, notifications are also sent to this address.
Optional. Incoming webhook URL for a Microsoft Teams channel. When set, time-off and cancellation notifications are also posted to Teams.
How to set up Teams to receive messages
  1. Open Microsoft Teams and go to the channel where you want notifications.
  2. Click the ⋯ (more options) next to the channel name.
  3. Choose Connectors or Incoming webhook.
  4. Click Add or Configure for “Incoming Webhook”. Name it (e.g. “Timesheet”) and create.
  5. Copy the generated webhook URL and paste it into the “Teams account” field above, then save.

If messages do not appear, check that the URL is correct, the webhook was not removed in Teams, and your network allows outbound HTTPS to outlook.office.com and *.webhook.office.com.
